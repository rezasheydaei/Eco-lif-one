How to Start a Zero-Waste Life with Small Challenges?

Think about this: how much waste do you produce every day? From food packaging to disposable coffee cups, it adds up quickly. Now imagine reducing that waste to nearly zero! A zero-waste lifestyle might sound daunting, but it starts with small, manageable steps.

In this article, we introduce fun and easy challenges to kickstart your zero-waste journey. Try a week without single-use plastics or switch to natural cleaning products. Carry a reusable bag or water bottle wherever you go. These small actions not only help the environment but also give you a sense of accomplishment.
